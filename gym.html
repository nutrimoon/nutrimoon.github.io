Deadbug

a) Lie on your back with your arms skywards and legs raised and bent at 90 degrees.
b) Slowly lower your arms behind you, as you extend your left leg out in front.
c) Exhale, then gently return to the starting position and repeat, this time extending the right leg. Lower the weight if your back arches off the floor. Do 10 reps per leg.

Barbell hip bridge

a) Sit on the floor with a bench behind you and a weighted barbell over your legs. Roll the bar so itâ€™s directly over your hips and lean back so your shoulder blades rest on the bench.
b) Drive through your feet, pushing up your hips (shoulders and feet supporting you). Squeezing your glutes, extend as high as possible. Hold for 5 secs then slowly lower. Do 20 reps.

Renegade row

a) With a dumbbell in each hand, start in a plank position, keeping your pelvis as stable as possible.
b) Row your right arm back without twisting your shoulders, keeping your elbows tucked in to engage your lats.
c) End with your wrist by your hip, then lower the dumbbell back to the floor.
d) Repeat on the other side, then do a press-up. That's one rep. Do 10 reps total.

Floor dumbbell Russian twist

a) Sit on the floor with your ankles together. Hold a dumbbell with both hands. Twist your arms to one side, clasping the dumbbell firmly.
b) Hold the dumbbell so you are nearly touching the floor. Embrace your core and do the same on the other side. Do 10 reps.
