What is a Keto diet?

Eating a keto diet usually means consuming 1,000 to 1,500 calories per day. This creates a calorie deficit that can lead to weight loss. This is a very effective way to lose weight, but it takes a lot of discipline and endurance to really work, not harm your health or lead to weight gain. Ideally, you should seek the help of a dietitian or doctor so that you don't limit your calories too much or miss out on essential nutrients.

How to properly organize a keto diet?

There are a number of rules that will allow you to organize a low-calorie meal without harming your health:

  • Do not reduce your energy intake to less than 200-300 calories from your basal metabolic rate;
  • Try to eat enough protein and fat;
  • Give preference to vegetable fats;
  • Try to choose lean meats and fish;
  • Eat only whole grain bread;
  • Eat at least 5 times a day - this will avoid severe hunger and reduce the risk of breakdowns during the addictive stage;
  • Do not be afraid of snacks, just calculate them right away and combine them with vegetables or unsweetened fruits.
